Recipe of Pizza

  • Equipments

    1. Pizza Oven
    2. Food Scale
    3. Pizza Cutters

  • Ingredients

    1. Dough
    2. Sauce
    3. Cheese
    4. Toppings

  • Procedure

    1. Take the Dough, add Cheese and Sauce
    2. Prepare oven at suitable temperature
    3. Add desired Toppings
    4. put pizza in oven for 15-20 mins
    5. Take out the pizza after it's crust is brown
