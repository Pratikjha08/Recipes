Recipe of Pasta

  • Equipments

    1. Knife
    2. A long-handled heat-proof spoon
    3. chopping board
    4. bowl
    5. A colander for draining the pasta

  • Ingredients

    1. 1 pound uncooked pasta
    2. A large (6- to 8-quart) pot
    3. Four to six quarts of cold water
    4. 1 teaspoon salt

  • Procedure

    1. Bring the water to a full, rolling boil.
    2. Before adding the pasta, add salt to the boiling water.
